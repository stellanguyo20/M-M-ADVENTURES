  1. Bush Excursions

Adventures in the wild comprises of not only game drives but also camping. Game drives generally occur in our country's national parks as well as reserves which include:

  • Nairobi National Park
  • Lake Nakuru National Park
  • Aberdare National Park
  • Samburu National Park
  • Amboseli National Park
  • Meru National Park
  • Tsavo East National Park
  • Maasai Mara Game Reserve
  • Tsavo West National Park

These game drives are exciting and very educative. Every group travelling with our company is assigned a guide who educates and guides our customers during these adventurous rides. Our tourists experience the wildlife's habitats and way of life from a personal view. The views are extraordinary, from the green pastures to the sloppy valleys, to the hills and valleys and most importantly the outstanding wildlife. Camping is an exciting adition to this wild experience where our tourist experience three night in the wild, sleeping to the sound of crickets and waking up to the birds singing. At night there is a bonfire where the campers meet and share their day's adventure with entertainment from the local people. Its a one in a million kind of experience.
