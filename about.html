Who Are We?

M&M Adventures Limited is a family based company founded in the year 2021. This company is an upcoming tour and travel company in Kenya focusing on local tourist with the hope of advancing to international travels in the upcoming years.

It is with great honour that we get to share the beautiful and expansive scenery of our nation Kenya with you our esteemed customers. Here at our company you will get to experience the following:

  • The Great Mountain Hikes
  • Scuba Diving
  • A Swim with the Dolphins
  • Nature Walks
  • Site Seeing in the county's Historic Sites
  • Game Drives
  • Camping in the Wild
  • Flight Scheduling
  • Team Building Activities
  • A Tour in country's National Parks and Reserves
